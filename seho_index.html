I am a Ph.D. student in Mathematics at the University of Wisconsin–Madison. My current research interest is in partial differential equations, with a focus on homogenization theory for Hamilton-Jacobi equations in infinite-dimensional settings.

Previously, I worked on mathematical modeling of biological systems, including causal inference in dynamical systems and mathematical sleep models, in collaboration with groups at KAIST and IBS.

Research interests

  • Partial differential equations
  • Homogenization theory
  • Mathematical biology and systems dynamics
  • Causal inference in biological systems
