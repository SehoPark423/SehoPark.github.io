Eunjee Kim

Ph.D. Student in Operations and Information Management
Wisconsin School of Business, University of Wisconsin–Madison
Advisors: Jordan Tong and Xiaoyang Long

My research lies at the intersection of behavioral operations management, human–AI decision-making, and experimental design. I use controlled laboratory and online experiments to study how information design, evaluation architecture, and cognitive biases shape decision quality — with a particular focus on hiring and multi-stage selection processes.

Research interests

  • Behavioral operations management
  • Social learning
  • Sustainable operations management
  • Human–AI interaction and decision-making
  • Hiring, evaluation, and fairness in selection
